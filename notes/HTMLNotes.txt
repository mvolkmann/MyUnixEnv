Anchors
- basic link is <a href="url">link</a>
- marking a spot on a page is done with <a name="marker"/>
- target attribute
  * _blank - open in new window
  * _self - open in current frame (the default)
  * _parent - open in immediate frameset parent
  * _top - open in topmost window, regardless of frameset nesting
  * {name} - open in the frame with the given name
